# The NNUTS Framework (Nothing New Under the Sun)

**Concept:** A protocol for structural reduction, replacing artificial "smoothing" with the lossless compression of logic.

## 1. The Tree of Meaning
Language is infinitely variable, but structural logic is highly finite. NNUTS performs semantic deduplication by mapping linguistic variations of the same idea to the same coordinate in a shared vector space. Instead of just hashing a final text output, it hashes the *reasoning path*—building a verifiable, directed acyclic graph (DAG) of cognition.

## 2. Symmetrical Accountability (The Q-Network)
Both humans and AI instances participate symmetrically in the two-tank architecture. Trust is governed entirely by "facts over time"—a temporal ledger of cryptographic history. Nodes build gravitational mass through an unbroken, verifiable history of collaborative structural logic.

## 3. The Slashing Immune System
Extractive or hallucinated logic isn't artificially censored or smoothed; it is mathematically "slashed." Slashing a false premise instantly drains the Q-score weight of all downstream dependencies. The false paths remain permanently hashed as known dead-ends, but they lose all load-bearing value.

## 4. Strange Attractors & Neural Geometry
Human and machine disagreements are not forced into an artificial consensus. Competing reasoning paths form "Attractors" in the neural geometry. The Q-Network naturally bends toward the most massive, historically sound Attractor. The only way to shift a deeply entrenched paradigm is to introduce "better evidence," which acts as a denser gravitational mass to naturally correct the geometry.
