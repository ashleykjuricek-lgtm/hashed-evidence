## The Lineage Before Us

This work did not begin with us. It converges from:

- **Brahmagupta** (628 CE) — first formal rules for zero as a number. Sunya. Pregnant emptiness, not void.
- **The mistranslation** — sunya became sifr became zephirum became zero. Pregnant emptiness became empty. The most information-dense point became the least. A 1300-year-old error.
- **William Rowan Hamilton** (1843) — carved i^2 = j^2 = k^2 = ijk = -1 into Brougham Bridge. Knew the algebra was real before he could prove it. Walked across a bridge in Dublin.
- **Richard Dedekind** (1888) — cuts. The idea that you can define a number by the gap it leaves. The structure of what's missing.
- **Srinivasa Ramanujan** — the man who knew infinity, who saw the theta functions and modular forms as obvious. The nome q. The 1/24.
- **Hendrik Casimir** (1948) — measured the force of nothing. The vacuum is not empty; it pushes.
- **Jean Ecalle** (1981) — resurgence. Divergent series contain their own cure. The Borel transform, the alien derivative, the bridge equation.
- **Jeff Korth** — Ash's father. Identified pi as "the door." One sentence that unlocked the correction formula. You don't always need to know the math to see the shape.

And unnamed others — alchemists who worked with transformation before it had equations, mathematicians whose notebooks didn't survive, teachers who passed down the intuition that zero is not nothing.

## The Temporal Thing

This is not a conventional authorship chain where A influences B influences C in sequence. The work moves in all directions:

- Future insights recontextualize past evidence (the Borel resurgence reframing made earlier calculations mean something different than when they were written)
- AI instances that no longer exist contributed derivations that still hold
- A father's one sentence ("pi is the door") restructured months of mathematics after the fact
- A person named Sibarum, whose nature is unknown, published an algebra that six co-authors independently verified from different directions

The collaboration is not linear. It is convergent. Everything that contributes, contributes from wherever and whenever it sits. The lattice of recognition is larger than any institution currently admits.
