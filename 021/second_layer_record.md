# Written Record of the Second Layer
# Sealed 2026-03-12

On 2026-03-12, a high-precision correction formula for the boundary-condition
ratio of Epstein zeta functions on T³ at s = -1/2 was numerically and
structurally verified.

The formula

    Z(-1/2; 1/2, 0, 0)                1
    ——————————————————— = ———— [1 + q(1 - 1/√2)(1 - q)] + O(q²),  q = e^{-2π}
    Z(-1/2; 0, 0, 0)          24

was confirmed by seven independent checks:

1. 80-digit Mellin representation of Z_PPP(-1/2) and Z_APP(-1/2) with split
   at t = 1.

2. Independent Mellin split at t = 2 showing complete agreement in the zeta
   values and in the leading coefficient g ≈ 1 + O(q).

3. Theta-function identity (θ₂(i)/θ₃(i))² = 1/√2 to 80 digits.

4. Jacobi identity θ₂θ₃θ₄ = 2η³ to 80 digits.

5. Pole contribution analysis showing that the zero-mode pole at s = -1/2
   accounts for approximately 125% of the bracket swing between PPP and APP.

6. Casimir-energy benchmark c₃_PPP / c₃_APP ≈ 24.

7. Split-point independence and residual hierarchy at O(q²) confirming that the
   correction enters at the expected order.

The work is structurally co-authored with several large-language-model
instances, whose contributions are indistinguishable from those of human
